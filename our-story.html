Our Story

Chef KK and the idea behind My Roti Place.

My Roti Place started with one simple promise: make butter chicken roti, biryani, and late-night comfort food feel just as dependable as pizza or burgers in Toronto.

Built around the tawa

Chef KK shaped the menu around fast-moving favorites that still taste like a real kitchen: hot rotis, rich curries, fragrant rice, crispy snacks, and drinks that round out the meal.

That is why the menu stays focused on craveable staples, fast pickup, and direct ordering that gets people fed without extra detours.

Made for Toronto timing

From lunch rushes to late-night cravings, My Roti Place was built to work across the whole day. Multiple stores stay open late, and the ordering flow is designed to make pickup feel easy.
